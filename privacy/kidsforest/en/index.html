[Kids Forest] Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2, 2026

justkeepgoing (hereinafter referred to as the "Service Provider" or "Kids Forest") values your privacy and complies with applicable privacy laws. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Purpose of Data Collection

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  1. User Management: To provide social login (OAuth), identify users, and prevent unauthorized use.
  2. Service Provision: To analyze child temperament, provide community features, and offer personalized content.
  3. Marketing & Advertising: To provide customized advertisements (e.g., Google AdMob) and improve service quality.

2. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to provide the service.

  • Retention Policy: All personal data is deleted immediately upon account withdrawal.
  • However, data may be retained if required by applicable laws for investigation purposes.

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information (Social Login): Provider ID (Google/Kakao), Email, Nickname, Profile Picture.
  • Child Information (Provided by Guardian): Name (or Nickname), Date of Birth, Gender, Temperament Test Results.
  • Automatically Collected Data: IP Address, Cookies, Device Info, Usage Logs, Advertising ID (ADID/IDFA).

5. Children's Privacy

This service is intended for use by guardians (parents). We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under the age of 14 without parental consent.

Any information regarding children (e.g., temperament data) is collected solely through the guardian's account and with their implicit consent. Guardians have the right to review or delete their child's information at any time by withdrawing from the service.

6. Security Measures

We implement technical and administrative measures to protect your data, including encryption of sensitive information and strict access controls.

7. App Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function properly:

  • Required: Internet Access (Network).
  • Optional:
    • Camera: To take profile or post photos.
    • Photo Library: To upload images to posts.
    • Microphone: For audio recording features (if applicable).

You may use the service without granting optional permissions, but some features may be restricted.

8. Child Safety Policy

Kids Forest prioritizes the safety of children and strictly adheres to Google Play’s Child Safety Standards.

  • Zero Tolerance for CSAM: We have a zero-tolerance policy towards Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM). Any content involving Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) is strictly prohibited. Such content will be removed immediately, and the offending user’s account will be permanently banned.
  • Reporting Mechanism: Kids Forest provides an in-app reporting feature that allows users to immediately report any inappropriate content or behavior related to child safety.
  • Legal Compliance: We comply with all applicable laws regarding child safety and will report serious violations to the relevant law enforcement and child protection authorities.
  • Inquiries: Any concerns or reports regarding child safety can be sent to our safety contact at justkeepgoing.dev@gmail.com.
